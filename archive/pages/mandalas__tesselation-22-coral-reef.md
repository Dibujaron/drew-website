# Tesselation #22: Coral Reef

This one was inspired by underwater scenes. It was my first attempt to use a new chalkboard after doing the first 21 tesselations on the same erased board. I over-seasoned the new board and as a result this one is too cloudy to keep, so it will be erased. This design came out very dense; most of the lines are very close together. I relaxed on this in later iterations.
