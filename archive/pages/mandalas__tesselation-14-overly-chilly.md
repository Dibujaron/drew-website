# Tesselation #14: overly chilly

This one is not remarkable; the heights of #8 had not yet been surpassed. On this one I was attempting to explore warmth and coolness, but I think I used too wide of a color palette, so it’s difficult to see the themes. The coolness on the bottom is apparent; it’s quite frigid actually, far too cold. On the other hand, the top fails to convey warmth; the dark green, white, and purple are too heavy. It just feels chaotic.
