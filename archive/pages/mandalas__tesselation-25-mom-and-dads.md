# Tesselation #25: Mom and Dad's

This was the third piece I made for Christmas of 2022; this one was for my parents. I only have one photo of it, which includes me in it and was taken moments after it was completed. Like #24; this was meant to be a classic example of the art form, with no boundaries being pushed. I took care in the choice of colors to have them match the setting of my parents’ entryway. They seemed pleased with it.
