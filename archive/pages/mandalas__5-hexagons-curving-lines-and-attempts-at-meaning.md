# Tesselation #5: hexagons, curving lines, and attempts at meaning

This was the first experiment with lines intentionally not straight, and also with 6-sided swirls. My favorite part of this one is the large, parallel vertical twists that come out of the hexagonal swirl in the bottom right. It was meant to be a very abstract landscape but I don’t think that really came through. It did end up with a nice contrast between a fairly-otherworldly “sky” and a “ground” of cooler earthier colors.
