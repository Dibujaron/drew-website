# Tesselations #23: Southwestern Buttes

I made chalk artwork as Christmas presents for my sister, my parents, and my grandmother for Christmas of 2022.

I took detailed progress pictures of the development of my sister’s, so I have more photos to share here than usual. It was a southwestern color scheme, and I tried to portray some landscape in it as well. The results were good, but ultimately I over-sprayed it with fixative, so the final product was very dark.
